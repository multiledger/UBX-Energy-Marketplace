This Blockchain dApp in the power energy sector replicates a marketplace where peers in a permission blockchain network exchange energy using tokens, and these energy tokens can be redeemed by rewards tokens with other companies merchandise or services. This network may have electric distribution companies, prosumers and consumers. The energy trade is based on tokens called REC-kWh.
1 REC-kWh token value is 1 kWh.

Please select your desired option from the menu on the left

  • Distributors/Prosumer: Add electric energy supplier
  • Consumer: Add electric energy consumer only
  • Transaction: Execute a transacton
  • Dashboard: View all transactons and manage your blockchain
